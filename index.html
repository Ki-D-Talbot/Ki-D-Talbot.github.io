Welcome to My Portfolio

This website showcases my university and personal projects as part of my studies at Staffordshire University. It serves as an assessed component of the module Robotic Modelling and Drone Skin Design (COMP60019).

Throughout this module, I have explored the integration of physical and virtual prototypes, focusing on biomimicry and soft robotics. My projects reflect my passion for the intersection of technology and nature, as well as my interest in compliant mechanisms.

Here, you will find detailed descriptions of my projects, including the techniques and technologies I have employed, as well as insights into my learning journey.
